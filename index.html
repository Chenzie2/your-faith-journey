The Good News

The world is broken, but there’s hope. God didn’t leave us in the dark. Out of love, He sent Jesus—fully God and fully human—to rescue us.

Jesus lived the perfect life we couldn't, died to take our place, and rose again to bring new life. Through Him, we can be forgiven, restored, and made new.

🛤️ What’s Next?

Congratulations on receiving eternal life! You’ve entered the narrow path—a journey with Jesus that leads to real life. This path isn’t about rules or religion, but a relationship. Let’s walk it together, step by step:

🙏 Prayer

Talk to God daily. Be real. He’s listening—always.

“Pray without ceasing...” — 1 Thessalonians 5:17

🌊 Baptism in Water

Go public with your faith. It shows the world what Jesus did for you.

“Repent and be baptized...” — Acts 2:38

🔥 Baptism in the Holy Spirit

Ask God to fill you with His power and presence.

“You will receive power...” — Acts 1:8

📖 God’s Word

Feed your faith daily through Scripture. Start with the Gospels.

“Man shall not live by bread alone...” — Matt. 4:4

🌱 Grow in Community

We grow stronger together. Find fellowship, not isolation.

“Crave pure spiritual milk...” — 1 Peter 2:2

❤️ Love God First

Put Jesus above everything. Love leads to real transformation.

“Love the Lord your God...” — Matt. 22:37

✨ Personal Revelation

Don’t just know about Jesus—get to know Him for yourself.

“That I may know Him...” — Phil. 3:10
