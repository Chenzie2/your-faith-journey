🗣️ Bear Witness

When the Holy Spirit comes upon us, it’s not just for comfort but for boldness. You were empowered for a purpose: to share Jesus with the world.

“But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes upon you. And you will be my witnesses… to the ends of the earth.”

This is the Great Commission; a command from Jesus to every believer. You carry the hope the world desperately needs. Don’t hold it back.
